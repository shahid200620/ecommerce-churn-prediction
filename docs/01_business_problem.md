\# Business Problem Statement



\## 1. Business Context

The e-commerce industry faces intense competition, where customers can easily switch to alternative platforms. Customer retention is critical because acquiring a new customer costs 5–25 times more than retaining an existing one. RetailCo Analytics is experiencing customer drop-offs and wants to proactively identify customers at risk of churn.



\## 2. Problem Definition

Customer churn is defined as:

> A customer who has not made any purchase in the last 90 days.



The objective is to predict which customers are likely to churn in the next 3 months using historical transaction data.



\## 3. Stakeholders

\- \*\*Marketing Team\*\*: Target at-risk customers with retention campaigns

\- \*\*Sales Team\*\*: Improve repeat purchase rates

\- \*\*Product Team\*\*: Understand purchasing behavior patterns

\- \*\*Executive Team\*\*: Measure revenue impact and ROI



\## 4. Business Impact

\- Expected churn reduction: 15–20%

\- Improved customer lifetime value

\- Reduced marketing costs through targeted outreach

\- Increased revenue from retained customers



\## 5. Success Metrics

\*\*Primary Metric\*\*

\- ROC-AUC ≥ 0.78



\*\*Secondary Metrics\*\*

\- Precision ≥ 0.75

\- Recall ≥ 0.70

\- F1-Score ≥ 0.72
